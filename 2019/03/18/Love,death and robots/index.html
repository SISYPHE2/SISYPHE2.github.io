Love,death and robots

2019-03-18

The new series of Netflix ‘Love,death and robots’ get pursued and admired by chinese audiences in recent days.The series has 18 episodes,and each episode tells

differents stories.After reviewing nine episodes,i must say that the episode is not as great as how people told us to be.However,there are more and more people

choose to fllow the trend,share review in their social media and rate highly in Douban.

The situtation is quiet like when another Netflix’s movie ‘roma’ got a hit in chinese audiences.As far as i seen,both plays is a good project,but they didn’t

suppose to get so much attention by the normal auidences.First of all,the background of the story is so far from us.Secondly,the cultural differences are such

a great barrier between us and the dirctor.Last but not least,we can’t deny that as a commerial stream media,the Netflix,always tend to make products which

were directed to its consumers.While,as we all konwn,the chinese audiences,for some reason,can’t be its consumers.

In a nutshell,it’s quiet odd for me that the Netflix’s plays get so much attention in chinese market.As a commerial organization,what the Netflix doing is

trying to make the ‘global topic’ in their consumers despite of the geographical and cultural differences.As chinese audiences,the great leap in both economy

and culture make them have a stronge desire to get into this ‘global topic’,which make the ‘Love,death and robots’ and ‘roma’ so popular in china.
